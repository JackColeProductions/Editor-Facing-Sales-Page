FAQ

Questions Clients Ask

Everything you need to know before posting your first job.

4.9/5 from 2,500+ clients
How are the editors vetted?

Every editor is portfolio-reviewed and rated on real, completed projects. You only ever see editors with a proven track record on the platform.

How much does it cost to post a job?

Posting a job and browsing editors is free. You only pay your editor's rate when you hire, plus a small platform fee on completed work.

How does escrow / payment protection work?

You fund the project into escrow up front, but the money is only released to the editor once you approve the final delivery. Your funds are protected the whole way through.

How fast will I get matched?

Most clients have qualified editors applying within hours of posting. The average time to match is under 24 hours.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

Revisions are built into every project, and because funds sit in escrow until you approve, you're never paying for work that doesn't meet the brief.

What types of editors are on VP+?

Short form, long form, YouTube, ads, documentary, real estate, ecom, education and more — across every niche and budget.

Can I hire for ongoing / retainer work?

Absolutely. Many clients start with a one-off project and convert their editor into a monthly retainer. You can hire for both right from the start.

Do I have to manage files and revisions myself?

No. Briefs, file delivery, revisions and version history all live inside the platform — no scattered email threads or lost links.
