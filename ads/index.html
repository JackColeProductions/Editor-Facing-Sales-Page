How fast will I actually get my ads?

24-48 hours from brief to delivery, per video. Because capacity fans out across hundreds of editors, ordering more ads doesn't slow anything down. 30 creatives ship as fast as 3.

What ad formats can I order?

Everything. AI ads (claymation, 3D animated, AI podcast style), AI UGC with hyper-real creators, and classic ads: UGC edits, direct-response video ads, hook variation packs, and repurposed winners. All from one dashboard.

How does pay-per-video pricing work?

Joining is free. You fund each video when you order it, the money sits in escrow, and it only releases to the editor when you approve the final ad. No subscription, no retainer, no minimums.

Who's actually editing my ads?

Elite editors from the VP+ marketplace, every one portfolio-reviewed and vetted before they can take work. They're matched to your brief by niche and work from Ad Creators Lab's ad creative frameworks.

What's the Ad Creators Lab connection?

This platform is a collaboration. Ad Creators Lab, the team behind $150M+ in managed ad spend and 1,000+ ads a month, brings the creative strategy and frameworks. VP+ brings 600+ elite, vetted editors. The best in the ad space, working with the best in the editing space.

What about revisions?

Revisions are built into the flow. Request them right on the video, with full version history. You don't release payment until you approve the final cut, so the incentives stay aligned.

Do I need to send footage?

For classic ads, send raw footage or existing assets and we cut from those. For AI ads and AI UGC, product shots and a brief are enough. The creative is generated, so there's nothing to film.

Can you really handle "infinite" volume?

Yes. That's the point of the model. Your briefs fan out across 600+ vetted editors working in parallel, so volume never queues behind one person's calendar. Order one ad or a hundred; the turnaround stays 24-48 hours.
