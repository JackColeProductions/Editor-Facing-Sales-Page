How does payment protection work?

Every project is escrow-funded. You add the budget upfront, but the editor doesn't get paid until you approve the final work. If they don't deliver, you don't pay. Simple.

How are the editors vetted?

Every editor on VP+ is reviewed before they can apply to jobs. We check their portfolio, past work, and niche specialty. No randoms. No undercutting bidders from sketchy accounts.

How fast will I get matched?

Most jobs start getting applications within minutes. You'll have a shortlist of editors who fit your brief within 12-24 hours, max.

How much does it cost to post a job?

Nothing. Posting is free. You only pay when you hire, plus a 3% processing fee on the project total. No subscriptions. No browsing fees.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

You don't release payment until you approve the final cut. If the work isn't right, you request revisions. If it's still wrong, the funds stay in escrow and our team helps resolve it.

What types of editors are on VP+?

Editors across every niche. YouTube long-form, short-form, podcasts, ads, documentary, ecommerce, fitness, gaming, finance, and more. Filter by niche when you post your job.

Can I hire for ongoing / retainer work?

Yes. Many clients use VP+ to find one editor and keep them on a monthly retainer. Post the job as ongoing and editors will apply with retainer rates.

Do I have to manage files and revisions myself?

No. Briefs, revisions, version history, and final delivery all live inside the platform. No email chains. No WeTransfer links going missing.
